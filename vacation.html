BENJI'S CASTLE

Welcome to our exquisite remote architectural home in the south of Kunaji, three miles from lake Masaya. An adventure for the modern creative. Dive into our ceramic studio, and work with fresh harvest clay. Enjoy the peaceful wilderness from dawn and dusk, while exploring a taste and wonders of island life. Don't hesitate to contact us today to book your memorable trip.

STUDIO

Personal studio

Sizeable fully-equipped enviromental friendly ceramic studio, with four pottery wheels and 30 pounds of fresh clay per guest. Modern electrical kilm in conjuction with a free art class from locally known ceramist Edward Johnaskin.

Exploration

While exploring, guest often want to take breaks and take it all in. The simplicity of their new home, and new horizon is enough to fulfil. Our ceramic studio a unique space merging the ceramic culture of our island,the spirit of exploration, and the adventage of proximity.
